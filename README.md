# Applied Econometrics Projects

This repository contains coursework completed for **ECO3010: Topics in Applied Econometrics**.

The projects apply causal inference methods to education and environmental policy questions using both experimental and quasi-experimental designs.

---

## Section A – Applied Empirical Analysis

### Exercise 1: Teacher Gender & Student Performance (RCT Framework)

Objective:  
Estimate the causal impact of teacher gender on student math test scores in disadvantaged schools.

- Randomised classroom assignment
- Interaction effects (student × teacher gender)
- Block fixed effects

---

### Exercise 2: Health Risk & Housing Prices (Panel & DiD)

Objective:  
Estimate the impact of a cancer cluster designation on residential property values.

- Parcel-level fixed effects (within transformation)
- Between vs within variation comparison
- Difference-in-Differences (Churchill vs Lyon)
- Pre/post treatment period analysis

----

## Section B – Research Design Exercise

Proposed a **Fuzzy Regression Discontinuity Design (RDD)** to estimate the causal effect of hazardous site cleanup on nearby residents’ health outcomes.

Includes:

- Instrumental variable framework
- Local identification around policy threshold
- Validity checks (manipulation test, bandwidth sensitivity, covariate continuity)
